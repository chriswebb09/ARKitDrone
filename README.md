# AR Drone Game Controller 🚁

Experience the thrill of piloting a drone right from your device! "AR Drone Game Controller" is an immersive Augmented Reality (AR) application designed to give users a realistic drone piloting experience. Built with ARKit and SceneKit, our controller simulates the dynamics of real-world drones.

![Screenshot](path_to_screenshot.png)

*Demo GIF or video here showcasing the app in action*

## Features 🌟

- 🕹️ **Dual Joysticks**: Two on-screen joysticks for precise drone movement.
- 🚀 **Missile Control**: Arm, disarm, and shoot missiles with ease.
- 🌌 **Dynamic Environment**: Realistic reflections with dynamic environment mapping.
- 🔍 **Advanced AR**: Real-time mesh reconstruction and plane detection for a lifelike AR experience.

## Dependencies 📦

- UIKit (vX.X.X or newer)
- SceneKit (vX.X.X or newer)
- ARKit (vX.X.X or newer)
- SpriteKit (vX.X.X or newer)
